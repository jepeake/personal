### Overview

Developed a Multi-Layer NN Mini-Library using NumPy, with Linear Layer, Activation, Training, & Preprocessing classes.

Implemented & Optimised a NN Regressor Model to estimate Housing Prices with a normalised MSE of 0.192.

Designed & Justified the NN Architecture, performing Training, Hyperparameter Tuning, & Evaluation.
